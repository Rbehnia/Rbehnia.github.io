# 
I received my Bachelors in Information Technology and my Masters in Computer Science from Multimedia University, Malaysia. I was a recipient of an FRGS grant during his time as a lecturer in Multimedia University and was awarded a Researcher Award. I also received an Outstanding Graduate Scholar award from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Oregon State University. My main research interests are signature schemes, privacy enhancing technologies and post-quantum secure cryptography.
