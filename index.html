Rouzbeh Behnia

Research Assistant

Oregon State University

Rouzbeh received his Bachelors in Information Technology and Masters in Computer Science from Multimedia University, Malaysia in 2010 and 2013, respectively. He was a recipient of an FRGS grant during his time as a lecturer in Multimedia University and was awarded a Researcher Award in 2016. In 2016, Rouzbeh received an Outstanding Graduate Scholar award from the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, Oregon State University.
